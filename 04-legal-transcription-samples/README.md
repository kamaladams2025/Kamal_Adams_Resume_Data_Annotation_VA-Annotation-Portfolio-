# Legal Transcription — Style-Guide Adherence Sample

## What this is
A short, **fully synthetic** mock deposition excerpt, formatted to standard verbatim legal-transcription conventions (CVL-style: speaker labels, verbatim filler retention, timestamp markers, inaudible/crosstalk notation), paired with a style-guide checklist showing the rule applied at each formatting decision.

**Note on source material:** No actual client transcripts, case names, or proprietary TranscribeMe training content are reproduced here, in line with client confidentiality requirements. The excerpt below is invented dialogue for demonstration purposes only.

## Why it matters
Legal transcription QA is rule-based precision work — consistent speaker tagging, exact verbatim retention (including false starts and filler words), and correct handling of overlapping speech or inaudible audio. This sample makes the rule-to-output mapping explicit rather than just showing finished text, which is what a style-guide-adherence reviewer actually checks.
